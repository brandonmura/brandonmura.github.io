The ICT field

The field of information technology deals with the application and innovation of technology to solve business problems and discover growth opportunities. Information technology is also one of today’s fastest-growing and most in-demand fields of work, so it’s easy to see why IT is one of the most popular areas of study for undergraduate students.


Information technology is a versatile field full of ample opportunities for aspiring professionals to pursue a career path that aligns with their background, career goals, and professional and personal interests. Every industry is reliant on technology, which means there are - and will continue to be - extensive job opportunities for professionals who understand the latest technology and have niche experience or interest in the industry of a potential employer.
