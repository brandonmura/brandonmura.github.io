The ICT field and studying at HZ:

I joined the ICT study at HZ University because of the deep love I have for computers and all to do with them. I've always been on a computer for my entire life and it was always a passion for me to further my understanding and knowledge of the smallest components that make up the computer, both hardware and software. Coming to HZ gives me the opportunity to take advantage of the great facilities and amazing teachers to propel myself to greater ICT heights and fulfil my love and passion for the subject. The field of information technology deals with the application and innovation of technology to solve business problems and discover growth opportunities. Information technology is also one of today's fastest-growing and most in-demand fields of work, so it's easy to see why IT is one of the most popular areas of study for undergraduate students.


You can not separate me from a computer, whether it be a phone or gaming station. I really enjoy trying and creating new codes and general programming. My passion coupled with the opportunity of being at HZ can only promise a really exciting future for me.
